About Machinery


Machinery Division

A prominent player, offering heavy earthmoving, construction and mining equipment to achieve customer excellence.

Machinery division has developed a complete understanding of the stringent industrial requirements of its customers and endeavors to deliver products that are best of the breed, known for their robust design, quality and reliability. Essential and fast-moving spare parts are sufficiently stocked in our inventory and the nationwide service network is there to meet the ongoing customer requirements for after-sales support. Our well-trained sales and service teams are all geared to provide their most professional services to our customers and Principals.

Over a period of time, ProTech Solutions (Private) Limited has successfully developed long-term business relations with a number of its esteemed Principals, partners and customers working in the areas of roads and bridges, infrastructure development, housing, agriculture/sugar, mining, oil and gas, power generation (such as hydel, thermal, coal, waste to energy, biomass, biogas, solar and wind), power transmission and distribution, automation, railways, ports and shipping, aviation, environment, water and sewage, waste management, etc., safety and security, health and education. Project & Machinery division has also developed a professional approach in providing comprehensive turnkey solutions and consultancy services to its esteemed customers in different fields of operation.

Regular foreign and local training programs are conducted to upgrade the expertise of our sales and technical staff. Training workshops and physical demonstrations are also conducted for customers to enhance their technical knowledge for effective usage of products.

ProTech Solutions (Private) Limited represents organizations of international repute from North America, Europe, Japan, China and other Asian countries that are market leaders in their areas of expertise. Machinery division selects Principals and partners that have a keen interest in offering their products and services to customers of ProTech Solutions (Pvt) Limited in Pakistan with a sincere approach of the best value for money, coupled with timely and efficient after-sales service.

ProTech Solutions (Private) Limited has established its setup in China to closely liaison with Principals/manufacturers of repute to engage them for development projects in Pakistan.
