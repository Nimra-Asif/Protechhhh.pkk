Corporate Social Responsibility

We, the ProTech Solution, consider the society and the communities we operate in as our valued stakeholders and take responsibility for the impact of those activities on all our stakeholders as well as the environment. We are also fully conscious of our social responsibilities. Corporate Social Responsibility is a full fledged corporate function looked after directly by a member of the Board of Directors.

Some are some of the causes that ProTech Solution is actively sponsoring and involved in for the good of our communities

The ProTech Solutions believe that healthcare is a fundamental human right and should be available to anyone and everyone, either affordable or free of cost. With this belief, the ProTech Solutions in 2000, ventured into setting up the Fatima Foundation in the Moach Goth area, off Hub River Road. The Fatima Foundation caters to the residents of Sindh and Baluchistan, especially the low-income areas, such as Baldia Town, Duad Goth, Musharraf Colony, Ittehad Town, Javed Bahria, S.I.T.E. Town, and Lasbella District. It is the only private secondary care hospital of its kind, addressing the healthcare needs of the surrounding underprivileged, rural and urban strata that virtually have no access to any other medical facility.

Fatima Foundation is a not-for-profit organization and is managed by the revenue it generates in addition to the help and support it receives from the ProTech Solutions and other individuals and organizations in the form of zakat and donations. The hospitalâ€™s facilities are inline with medical standards and services include OPD, General and Private Wards, Intensive Care Units, Neonatal Intensive Care Unit, Operating Theatres, Radiology, Ultrasound, Blood Bank and 24 hours open Laboratory, Pharmacy and Accident & Emergency wing with surgical facility. At Fatima Foundation, no patient is turned away or asked to pay for the services if they are unable to do so, medical care is provided at subsidized rates or through its Patient Welfare Association.
